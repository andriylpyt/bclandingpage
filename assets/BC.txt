Blas Cantó knows what life is like on stage. Since he became known, less than 10 years old, in national talent contests, the Murcian artist has not stopped working.

The explosion of fame came when he was part of the group, with which they released 4 studio albums, and became the biggest fan phenomenon of recent decades in Spain.

After the band's dissolution, Blas becomes the winner of "Tu cara me suena" and releases "Complicado (Edición Deluxe)", his first solo work, which has reached number 1 of the best sellers in Spain.

Songs like "In your bed", "Si te vas" and, above all, "Él no soy yo" (which went triple platinum, number 1 in the Top 40 and one of the best-selling songs of the year) make Blas an artist of multidisciplinary success.

With the arrival of the pandemic, the artist is the representative of Eurovision for two consecutive years, with "Universo" and "Voy a quedarme".

After two years of personal darkness, in which melancholy and sadness were reflected in the music and aesthetics of the singer, he now returns to his optimistic and colorful pop with "El Bueno Acaba Mal", the first preview of what will be his long-awaited second studio album.
